Hands on Engineering

In our course we will use concepts like the Rube Goldberg Machine, Balloon Car, and the Catapult in order to make it simpler for students to learn about more complicated engineering topics

Structural Design

In the Structural Design part of the course students will learn about weight distribution, cushioning, and stability when it comes to designing bridges and other things.

Robotics

The course will teach students the basics of robotics and circuitry. They will learn how circuits work with the flow of electricity and how to make simple robots that can do simple tasks

Aerodynamics

Students will learn about Aerodynamics through the concepts of parachutes and rockets. They will learn about drag force, propulsion, lift, and many other concepts

Biology

In the Biology section, students will learn about DNA and how it shapes the attributes of you as a person and many other things. Through a simple experiment with strawberries students will be able to see DNA.

Chemistry

In Chemistry section, students will learn about density. They will learn about what it is and the many different applications there are.

Optics

In the Optics part of our program students will learn about light, our eyes, through the use of kaleidoscopes. This will help them understand how cameras and other visual inventions work.
